## How to log in and access your data

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Gigasheet and log in">
    Open the <a href="https://app.gigasheet.com/spreadsheet/RevenueBase-File/5f776238_022d_46c4_b0f1_8f6cfff75132" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">RevenueBase file in Gigasheet</a> (or use your Gigasheet URL from your admin). Log in with your credentials. If you use SSO or a custom domain, use the sign-in option your organization has configured.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Shared with me">
    Under **Workspaces**, open the **Shared with me** section. Your RevenueBase file is listed there.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your RevenueBase file">
    Click the RevenueBase file to open your licensed dataset (e.g. people + company or enhanced view). You can then search, [filter](/gigasheet/filtering), [save views](/gigasheet/saving-views), [export](/gigasheet/exporting), and [enrich your data](/gigasheet/enrichment).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**Prefer to browse from the app?** You can also go to <a href="https://gigasheet.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Gigasheet</a>, log in, and navigate to **Workspaces** → **Shared with me** to find the RevenueBase file.
</Tip>

<Warning>
If you don't have a Gigasheet login or don't see RevenueBase data in **Shared with me**, contact your account manager or admin to confirm your subscription and access. You can also contact **support@revenuebase.ai**.
</Warning>
