Send RevenueBase data from Gigasheet into your CRM or other systems.

## HubSpot integration

You can push RevenueBase data directly from Gigasheet into HubSpot — whether you want to update existing HubSpot records or add new ones.

### How to push data to HubSpot

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a segment">
    In Gigasheet, create a segment (filtered view) of the data you want to push.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save to HubSpot">
    Go to **File** → **Save to** → **HubSpot**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect HubSpot">
    When prompted, sync your HubSpot API key and complete the connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Push data">
    Once connected, data flows one-way out of Gigasheet into HubSpot.
  </Step>
</Steps>
